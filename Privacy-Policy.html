Privacy Policy

For now, we don't collect any personal data. If information/data is not collected or retrieved, it cannot be demanded, stolen, leaked or abused. Protecting your personal data is the best way to safeguard your online privacy.

How we define "personal data"

Personally identifiable information (PII) is any information related to an identifiable person such as information relating to Name, Personal identification numbers, Personal address information, Personal telephone numbers, Personal characteristics, Biometric data, Information identifying personally owned property and Asset information.

Information we don’t collect

So let’s be transparent here:
